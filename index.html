Join Our Preferred Network

A circle of agents who actually understand the 203k — and get rewarded for it.

What it is

Team Rizzolo has built its name closing loans the right way — effectively, efficiently, and especially when it comes to 203k and renovation financing. The Preferred Network is the circle of agents who get it: agents who understand that a renovation loan is often the easier path, who know the process, and who don't flinch when one crosses their desk. Our seminars, calls, and trainings are how you join that circle — and set yourself apart from agents who shy away from deals they don't understand.

What it means for your listings

Once you know the 203k, your listings do too. Homes listed by Preferred Network agents are marked 203k-Friendly — a signal to every buyer and buyer's agent that the property is a strong candidate for renovation financing. Those listings go into a shared database that other agents search when they're helping a client find a home that fits a 203k.

What you get

  • Training that genuinely sets you apart from set-in-their-ways agents
  • Your listings flagged 203k-Friendly and added to the shared database
  • Exposure to other network agents actively looking for renovation-ready homes
  • Buyers who come to us searching for exactly these properties
  • A lending team that actually closes renovation loans — on time

How it works

  1. Attend the seminars, calls, and trainings — learn the 203k inside and out
  2. Show us you understand the process and won't shy from a renovation deal
  3. Your listings earn the 203k-Friendly badge and join the database
  4. Get in front of more agents and more buyers — and close more homes

🏚️The 203k Way

Renovation loans, done right. We turn "needs work" homes into closings — so your sellers net more, your buyers have more options, and your business grows.

Sellers net more

A home that needs work draws lowball investors. A 203(k) buyer is an owner-occupant who'll pay more to live there — so the listing sells for more, and faster.

Buyers get more options

Your buyers can purchase a home in almost any condition — dated or distressed — and finance the repairs into one loan. More inventory, more deals.

Realtors grow their business

Build a contractor network you can refer, move the listings others can't sell, and rescue deals that would otherwise fall apart over condition.
