Team Rizzolo · Loan Officer Playbook

The Loan Application Question Tree

Run the call in this order — purpose first, because it shapes every question after it. Follow each branch only when the answer opens it. By stage 11 the borrower's document list has written itself.

Yes follow the branch No move on Option pick the path that fits Flag slow down — set expectations now
1

Loan purpose "What are we trying to do today?"

  • Purchase
    • Under contract? Yes price · closing date · contract copy · agent  No pre-approval: price range · area · timeline
    • Down payment? under 20% → MI talk; check FHA / VA / USDA / Conv 97 fit · gift or borrowed funds → stage 6
    • Occupancy? primary · second home (no rental income) · investment → using rent to qualify? market-rent analysis
    • Property type? SFR · condo (dues? warrantable? litigation?) · 2–4 unit (owner-occupying? leases?) · manufactured (titled as real property?) · new build
    • Needs renovation? Yes scope · budget · structural or cosmetic → Limited vs Standard 203(k) / HomeStyle · contractor picked?
  • Refinance
    • Goal? rate/term → current rate · balance · payment · loan type (FHA streamline? VA IRRRL?)  ·  cash-out → how much · for what (debt payoff → get the list now) · estimated value
    • Second lien, HELOC, or solar on the home? Yes balance · payoff or subordinate · solar: owned, leased, or PPA?
    • Listed for sale in the last 6 months? Yes Flag seasoning issue
2

Borrowers "Who's going to be on the loan?"

  • Co-borrower? Yes run the whole tree for them too · married to each other? · occupying? No non-occupant co-borrower rules
  • Each borrower: legal name (as on ID) · DOB · SSN · contact info
  • Marital status? separated → court-ordered support? → stage 7  ·  dependents & ages (VA residual income)
  • U.S. citizen? No permanent resident? Yes green card  No visa type / EAD → program eligibility
  • First-time buyer? Yes DPA programs · homebuyer ed · FTHB pricing
3

Current housing "Where do you live now?"

  • Address — how long? under 2 years → prior addresses until 2 years covered
  • Rent monthly rent · landlord contact (VOR) · paid electronically? (12-mo history helps thin credit)
  • Own
    • Plan at closing? selling (listed? contingent? net proceeds → stage 6) · renting it (lease lined up? → stage 8) · keeping vacant Flag both payments count in DTI
    • Mortgaged? Yes payment? → includes taxes & insurance? Yes done  No annual taxes + insurance   No annual taxes + insurance
    • HOA dues? HELOC or second lien? Yes amounts · balance · drawn?
  • Rent-free with whom · how long · homeowner letter (FHA)
4

Employment & income "How do you get paid?"

  • W-2 employer · title · start date (under 2 yrs → prior job or school; gaps 30+ days → LOX)
    • Salary or hourly? hourly + fluctuating hours → 2-year average
    • OT, bonus, or commission? Yes 2-year history? Yes average it  No Flag quote without it
    • Work for family or own part of the company? Yes tax returns — treat as self-employed
  • Self-employed (25%+ ownership) business type · 2+ years? No Flag 1–2 yrs + same-field W-2 maybe; under 1 yr → non-QM
    • Entity type? → which returns: 1040s + business returns + K-1s + YTD P&L
    • Heavy write-offs? Yes Flag the net qualifies — set expectations; bank-statement fallback
  • Retired Social Security (gross up non-taxable) · pension (for life?) · drawing retirement accounts? Yes continues 3+ yrs? balance supports it  No asset depletion option
  • Other say it: "You don't have to reveal alimony, child support, or separate maintenance unless you want it considered." child support / alimony (court order · 3-yr continuance · receipt history) · rental (→ stage 8) · disability / VA (award letter) · 2nd job (2-yr history) · military BAH/BAS (gross up)
5

Military service "Have you or a spouse ever served?"

  • Yes branch · dates · discharge type → COE / DD-214 · entitlement used before? restored? subsequent-use funding fee · disability rating? 10%+ → funding fee waived · surviving spouse → possible eligibility
  • No move on
6

Assets "Where's the down payment and closing money coming from?"

  • Each account: institution · balance · whose name
  • Own funds 2 months' statements · large or irregular deposits? Yes source and paper-trail each one
  • Gift from whom (acceptable donor?) · amount · gift letter + donor ability · wire or check
  • Home sale estimated net proceeds · CD at closing  ·  Asset sale bill of sale + proof of receipt  ·  401(k) loan terms (401k loan ≠ DTI debt)
  • Flag borrowed funds (generally not allowed unless secured) · cash on hand (needs seasoning — address now)
  • Earnest money paid? amount · which account cleared  ·  Reserves after closing? (required for investment / multi-unit / REO-heavy)
7

Liabilities "What do you pay every month?"

  • Car payments & leases (leases always count) · credit card balances
  • Student loans? deferred / IBR → program-specific payment calc (FHA 0.5% rule)
  • Child support or alimony you pay? amount · months left (under ~10 mo may exclude)
  • Co-signed for anyone? Yes 12 months' proof the other party pays → may exclude
  • Anything not on credit? private / family loans · IRS payment plan (agreement + payments made) · 401(k) loans (explains paystub deductions)
  • Paying off debts with this loan? Yes list which (cash-out refi)
8

Other real estate "Do you own any other properties?"

  • No move on
  • Yes how many? — then for each property: address · type · value
    • How is it used? rental → rent received · lease? · on tax returns? Yes Schedule E governs  No lease + 75% of gross rent  ·  second home (full payment counts, no offset) · vacant / land (carrying costs still count) · selling (before or after this closing?)
    • Mortgaged? Yes payment? → includes taxes & insurance? Yes done  No annual taxes + insurance + flood   No annual taxes + insurance
    • HOA dues? amount  ·  How many financed properties total? → conventional limits · 2–6 mo PITIA reserves each
9

Credit "Anything I should know before I pull credit?"

  • Get authorization (say whether soft or hard) · where do you think your score sits?
  • Ever filed bankruptcy? Yes chapter · discharge date → waiting periods (Ch7: FHA 2 yr / Conv 4 yr; Ch13 shorter or in-plan FHA)
  • Foreclosure, short sale, deed-in-lieu? Yes date → waits (FC: FHA 3 yr / Conv 7 yr)
  • Late mortgage or rent in last 12 months? · collections, charge-offs, judgments, tax liens? (judgments/liens must be paid or on plan; medical usually ignorable)
  • Disputed accounts? (may block AUS) · credit frozen? → unfreeze first · new debt lately — car, solar, furniture?
10

Declarations the gotcha catcher — every "yes" gets a follow-up

  • Outstanding judgments? · party to a lawsuit? (lien risk) · delinquent on federal debt? Yes Flag CAIVRS blocks govt loans — payment plan?
  • Borrowing any money for this deal not yet disclosed? · another mortgage application in process? · co-signer or guarantor on anything? (cross-check stage 7)
  • Owned property in last 3 years? (type · how titled → FTHB status) · intent to occupy as primary? confirm plainly · relationship with the seller? Yes non-arm's-length → scrutiny · gift-of-equity option
11

Wrap-up & logistics

  • How will you hold title? individual · joint · trust (trust docs) · LLC Flag most conforming loans won't allow
  • Anyone on title who isn't on the loan? · target closing date · hard deadlines (lease ending, lock horizon)
  • Escrow taxes & insurance, or pay yourself? (waiver pricing, LTV limits) · HMDA demographics (must ask, optional to answer) · eConsent + credit auth

The doc list writes itself

Every YES branch maps straight to a document. Read the borrower their exact needs list before you hang up.

gift → gift letter rental → lease + Schedule E self-employed → 2 yrs returns + P&L VA → COE + DD-214 selling home → contract + net sheet IRS plan → agreement + proof of payments
